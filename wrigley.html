About

Wrigley Field is located on the North Side of Chicago, Illinois. Wrigley Field is the home of the Chicago Cubs and also hosts a variety of concerts in the summertime. Tours of the field are also available.

Wrigley Field offers a number of services to people with disabilities. Below is a list of some of the services they offer that can found on their website.

  • Shuttle/Golf cart Service
  • Assistive Listening Devices
  • Elevators
  • Designated Parking Spots
  • Wheelchairs

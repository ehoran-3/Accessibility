About

Soldier Field is located on the lakefront in Chicago, Illinois. Soldier field is the home of the Chicago Bears and the Chicago Fire just recently relocated to Soldier Field. The venue also hosts concerts during the summertime.

Services Offered

Soldier Field offers a number of services to people with disabilities. Below is a list of some of the services they offer that can found on their website.

  • Hearing Assitance
  • Dropoff/Pickup Areas
  • Elevators
  • Designated Parking Spots
  • Wheelchairs

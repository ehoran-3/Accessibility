About

United Center is located in Chicago, Illinois. This venue hosts a variety of events ranging from concerts to sporting events. It is also home to the Chicago Blackhawks and the Chicago Bulls.

Services Offered

United Center offers a number of services to people with disabilities. Below is a list of some of the services they offer that can found on their website

  • Wheelchair Escorts
  • Accessible Entrances
  • Elevators
  • Designated Parking Spots
  • Hearing and Visual Assitance

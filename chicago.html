About

Chicago Theater is located centrally in downtown Chicago, Illinois. The venue hosts a variety of events including concerts, broadway plays and comedy shows.

Services Offered

Chicago Theater offers a number of services to people with disabilities. Below is a list of some of the services they offer that can found on their website.

  • Wheelchair Escorts
  • Wheelchair Storage
  • Elevators
  • Interpreted Performances
  • Assistive Listening Devices

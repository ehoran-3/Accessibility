About

Allstate Arena is located in Rosemont, Illinois right near O'Hare airport. This venue hosts a variety of events ranging from concerts to Chicago Wolves Hockey Games.

Services Offered

The arena offers a number of services to people with disabilities. Below is a list of some of the services they offer as listed on their website.

  • Wheelchair Escorts
  • Accessible Tickets
  • Designated Parking Spots
  • Sign Language Interpreters

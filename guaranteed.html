About

Guaranteed Rate Field is located on the South Side of Chicago, Illinois. Guaranteed Rate Field is the home of the Chicago White Sox and occasionally hosts concerts in thesummertime. The most recent concert hosted here was Chance the Rapper input 2016.

Guranteed Rate Field offers a number of services to people with disabilities. Below is a list of some of the services they offer that can found on their website.

  • Accessible Parking
  • Assistive Listening Devices
  • Accessible Seating
  • Elevators
  • Wheelchairs

Music and Sports Venue Accessibility

Purpose

Welcome to Accessibilty in Music and Sports Venues! The purpose of this webpage is to help people with disabilities find music/sports venues that are accessible in Chicago, Illinois. Each page on this site will focus on one specific venue and give details on the accessibilty for that venue. In addition, I have included a page comparing accessibility services between the venues. I created this page so that everyone can enjoy the thirll of concerts and live sporting events. I also created this page so that the information was easily accessible for all. I wanted the information to be all in one place so that people did not have to visit multiple sites to find the information they were looking for. I hope you find this site helpful!
